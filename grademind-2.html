FAQ

Common
questions.

How accurate is the marking?
Our AI is calibrated against real AQA-marked scripts and typically lands within 1–2 marks of a human examiner. Like any marking tool, it should be used as high-quality feedback, not as a guaranteed prediction of your final mark. The more you use it, the better you'll understand what the mark scheme rewards.
Does it work for all AQA Psychology topics?
Yes — all seven topics are fully covered: Social Influence, Memory, Attachment, Psychopathology, Approaches in Psychology, Biopsychology, and Research Methods. Each topic has its own knowledge bank so the AI marks with subject-specific precision.
Which question types does it mark?
Currently 16-mark "discuss" and "outline and evaluate" essays. 12-mark and 8-mark questions are in development and will be added in the next update.
Is my essay data stored or shared?
Your essays are processed to generate feedback and are not sold or shared with third parties. We retain anonymised essay data to improve the marking system. You can request deletion of your data at any time from your account settings.
Can my school or college use GradeMind?
Yes — we have a School plan with a teacher dashboard, class-wide progress tracking, and discounted per-student pricing. Email schools@grademind.co.uk and we'll set up a free trial for your department.
